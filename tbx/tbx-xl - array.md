# Excel Array Fomula Toolbox

Formula execution: `Ctrl+Shift+Enter`

| Action             | Syntax                                | Example                               |
| ---                | ---                                   | ---                                   |
| Product of two arrays`*` -> `array[float]`  | `=[range]*[range]`   | `=$C$2:$C$11*$D$2:$D$11`      |
| Sum the product of two arrays -> `float`  | `=SUM([range]*[range])`   | `=SUM(C2:C11*D2:D11)`      |

`*`: fix the ranges by `$`
